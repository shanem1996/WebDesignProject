Hi, I'm Melanie and I'm a makeup artist. Welcome to my website! After school I stumbled upon makeup, an interest I'd never really had before. I found it an amazing outlet, a thing to get lost in and find a creative part of me.

I like to do many looks but my favourite thing is to enhance the beauty my clients already have, by accentuating some of their best features!

Smokey eyes are my speciality. "The eyes are the window to the soul" so why not make them captivating!
